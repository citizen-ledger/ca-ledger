Sources & method

Figures are enacted-budget expenditures: appropriations under California's Budgetary-Legal basis of accounting, as published by the Department of Finance at ebudget.ca.gov when each year's Budget Act was signed. They are the spending plan fixed at enactment, not audited actual spending; mid-year revisions and actuals differ. General, special, and bond funds make up the state total; federal pass-through funds are shown via the toggle; reimbursements and nongovernmental-cost funds are excluded, matching how budget documents present state spending. "Per resident" divides the displayed total by the Department of Finance population estimate (report E-4) for that fiscal year. A new enacted budget is published each June — run pipeline/fetch_state_data.py then; it rewrites data.js in place. City-level data (State Controller's Office annual reports) is planned for V2.
