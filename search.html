One box across every layer: cities, counties, school districts, county offices, charter schools, special districts, community-college districts, CSU, UC, and the state budget's agencies and departments. Type a place and see every government that files under that name.

THESE LAYERS DO NOT ADD A city and the county that contains it serve overlapping populations with different responsibilities. A school district, a community-college district and a special district may cover the same ground again, and the state budget funds much of all of it. The figures behind these results are measured on different accounting bases — enacted appropriations, reported actuals, unaudited actuals, audited GAAP, as-filed — and are drawn from different sources for different years. Results are grouped by layer, each with its own basis stated, and are never summed or ranked against each other. This search finds records; it does not compare them.
